Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can you start?

For most projects, we can begin within 2–3 weeks of signing. We maintain a short waitlist to ensure availability.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes, but selectively. We typically engage from Seed stage up. We need to see conviction in the market and a clear path to product-market fit.

Who owns the code?

You do. Always. Full IP transfer is included in every engagement. We also provide complete documentation and handoff support.

Do you offer fixed-price projects?

We prefer time-and-materials with a monthly cap. Fixed-price projects reward slow discovery — we'd rather move fast and adjust.

Can we hire your engineers?

We have a no-poaching policy during engagements. After a project closes, we'll facilitate introductions if both parties are interested.

What happens after the project ends?

We offer retainer and advisory packages for post-launch support. Many clients keep us on at reduced capacity as their product scales.
