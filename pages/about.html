Our story

We are Nexus

Founded in 2019 by a team of ex-Google, Stripe, and Palantir engineers who believed the best software in the world shouldn't be locked behind VC darlings.

Built on conviction, not compromise

We started Nexus with a simple belief: great engineering is the highest leverage activity in a technology business. Not marketing. Not fundraising. Engineering.

Every founder we work with gets our full attention and our best engineers. We've never over-hired, never taken VC money, and never chased vanity metrics.

We're profitable, independent, and obsessive about craft. That's not a pitch — it's just who we are.

Craft over convenience
We do things right the first time, even when it's harder. Technical debt is a tax we refuse to pass to our clients.
Radical transparency
Daily updates, open access to our tools, and honest assessments — even when the news is bad.
Skin in the game
We've taken equity in several clients because we believe in what they're building. We only work on things we'd bet on.
Long-term thinking
We turn down projects that would damage the client relationship 12 months from now. Repeatability is our growth strategy.

Our history

How we got here

2019
Founded in a Dogpatch garage
James and Priya quit their jobs on the same day and started Nexus with two laptops, one client, and a very optimistic runway calculation.
2020
First enterprise client — and a pandemic
Signed our first enterprise contract ($800K ARR) the week lockdowns began. Went fully remote, hired six engineers, and delivered on time.
2021
The MediFlow HIPAA project
Spent 9 months building a healthcare platform that now serves 2 million patients. This project defined our approach to compliance-first engineering.
2022
AI practice launched
Hired Alex Rivera and built out our ML/AI capability. First year: 8 AI projects, $4M revenue, zero failed deployments.
2023
Turned down acquisition offer
A major consultancy offered $45M to acquire Nexus. We declined. Independence matters more than the exit.
2024
Finterra AI — our biggest project yet
Deployed a team of 8 for 14 months to rebuild the core intelligence layer of a $400M AI company. Shipped on time, on budget.
2025–26
48 people. Still bootstrapped. Still selective.
We've grown carefully, never over-hiring. Every Nexus engineer is a senior engineer. We take on fewer than 6 engagements per year, and we intend to keep it that way.
