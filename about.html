Our story

About Nexus

We started in 2019 with a simple belief: the best software is built by engineers who give a damn about the product.

Nexus was founded by three engineers who were tired of watching great ideas die at the hands of mediocre execution. We'd seen it too many times — startups with the right idea, the right market, the right timing — undone by technical debt, poor architecture, and teams that shipped features instead of outcomes.

We built the studio we wished we'd had access to. Senior engineers only. No junior-heavy body shops. No offshoring the hard parts. Every team member who works on your product has shipped production systems at scale.

We stay small on purpose. 4–6 engagements at a time. We're not trying to be the biggest — we're trying to be the best call you ever made.

What we believe

Our values

Craft over speed

We move fast. But we never cut corners that create technical debt. Every line of code we ship, we'd be proud to have our name on.

Outcomes over output

We don't count story points. We count results. If a feature doesn't move the metric, we'll tell you before we build it.

Radical transparency

Bad news doesn't get better with time. We surface problems immediately, communicate daily, and never sugarcoat estimates.

Senior engineers only

Every engineer on your project has 8+ years of experience. We don't learn on your dime — we've already learned the hard lessons.

Your IP, always

Everything we build belongs to you. Full code ownership, full documentation, built to be maintained by your team after we're gone.

Small on purpose

We deliberately cap engagements so every client gets our best. We've turned down big contracts to protect quality. We'd do it again.
