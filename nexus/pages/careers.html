Our hiring process

What to expect

STEP 01
Application review (3 days)
We read every application. No ATS black holes. A real human will respond within 3 business days, even if the answer is no.
STEP 02
30-min intro call
A casual conversation with your hiring manager. We'll tell you about the role, you'll tell us about yourself. No trick questions.
STEP 03
Technical interview (90 min)
We'll work through a real problem together — one we've actually solved at Nexus. No LeetCode trivia. We pay $200 for your time regardless of outcome.
STEP 04
Team interviews (2 hours)
Meet the people you'd work with. We do culture-fit assessment both ways — you're evaluating us too.
